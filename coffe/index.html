HOW IT TASTES

A classic herbal tea to savor by the sip any time of the day, this cup offers soothing chamomile flowers with subtle hints of sweet orange and tangy lemon. As an alternative, try it on ice during the summer to refresh and revive. Also, it makes an excellent after-dinner digestive. Pair the infusion with light foods and complementary flavors like cookies or fruit flavored item such as lemon or orange.

HISTORY

Chamomile has been enjoyed since at least Roman times. But this blend dates only to the late 1990's, when Mighty Leaf Tea founders Jill Portman and Gary Shinner introduced it to their fledgeling line. It has since become our most popular herbal tea blend.
